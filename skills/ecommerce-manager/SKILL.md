# 🛒 E-commerce Manager

_Manage online stores (Shopify, WooCommerce)_

## 🎯 Purpose

Manage e-commerce operations:
- Product management
- Order tracking
- Inventory management
- Sales analytics
- Customer management

## 🛍️ Features

### 1. **Product Management**
- Add/edit products
- Bulk operations
- Image management
- Variant handling

### 2. **Order Management**
- Order tracking
- Fulfillment
- Refunds/returns
- Customer notifications

### 3. **Inventory**
- Stock tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Auto-reorder
- Warehouse management

### 4. **Analytics**
- Sales reports
- Revenue tracking
- Customer analytics
- Product performance

## 💬 Usage

```
"Add new product: [details]"
"Show orders from today"
"Check inventory for SKU123"
"Generate sales report for March"
```
