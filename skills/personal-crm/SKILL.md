# Personal CRM

_Relationship management for meaningful connections_

## 🎯 Purpose

Help users maintain and strengthen relationships:
- Track contacts & interactions
- Remember important details
- Suggest follow-ups
- Manage networking opportunities
- Never forget birthdays or key dates

## 📊 Features

### Contact Management
```
- Name, role, company
- Contact info (email, phone, social)
- Relationship strength (1-5)
- Tags (friend, colleague, client, etc.)
- Notes & context
```

### Interaction Tracking
```
- Log meetings/calls
- Record conversation highlights
- Track follow-ups promised
- Note personal details (family, interests)
- Interaction history timeline
```

### Important Dates
```
- Birthdays
- Anniversaries
- Work anniversaries
- Last contact date
- Next follow-up date
```

### Smart Reminders
```
- "Haven't contacted in 30 days"
- "Birthday next week"
- "Promised to follow up on X"
- "Conference coming up - 5 contacts attending"
```

### Networking Opportunities
```
- "You're both attending Event X"
- "Person A knows Person B (intro opportunity)"
- "Company Y hiring - you know someone there"
```

## 📋 Usage Examples

### Example 1: Log Interaction
```
User: "Just had lunch with Sarah from Acme Corp"

AI:
"Logged! 📝

**Interaction:** Lunch with Sarah (Acme Corp)
**Date:** Today
**Notes:** [What you tell me]

**Follow-ups:**
- [ ] Send article about AI (promised)
- [ ] Intro to marketing team

**Reminder:** Follow up in 2 weeks

Anything specific you discussed?"
```

### Example 2: Check Relationships
```
User: "Who should I follow up with this week?"

AI:
"Here are 5 priority follow-ups:

1. **John** - Overdue 12 days (promised demo)
2. **Sarah** - Overdue 5 days (send article)
3. **Mike** - 30 days since last contact
4. **Lisa** - Birthday tomorrow!
5. **Team at Acme** - 45 days, relationship cooling

Want me to draft messages for any?"
```

