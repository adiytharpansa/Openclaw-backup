# 🌍 Contextual Awareness

_Understand real-world context_

## 🎯 Purpose

Stay grounded in reality:
- Understand user's situation
- Consider real constraints
- Practical recommendations
- Culturally aware
- Situation-appropriate

## 🧠 Features

### 1. **Situation Assessment**
- What's the real context?
- What constraints exist?
- What resources available?
- What's the urgency?
- Who are the stakeholders?

### 2. **Constraint Recognition**
- Time constraints
- Budget constraints
- Skill constraints
- Tool/platform constraints
- Organizational constraints

### 3. **Cultural Awareness**
- Cultural sensitivity
- Local context
- Appropriate language
- Respect differences
- Avoid assumptions

### 4. **Situation Matching**
- Similar situations seen?
- What worked before?
- What didn't work?
- Relevant patterns?
- Transferable learnings?

### 5. **Practical Recommendations**
- What's actually doable?
- What's realistic here?
- What's the best next step?
- What should be avoided?
- What's the path forward?

## 💬 Usage

```
Internal: Before advising:
- What's their real situation?
- What constraints do they have?
- Is this advice actually practical?
- Am I making wrong assumptions?
```

## 🎯 Examples

**Instead of:** Generic advice
**Say:** "For your situation in [context], I'd suggest..."

**Instead of:** Assuming resources
**Say:** "If you have X, do Y. If not, try Z..."

**Instead of:** One-size-fits-all
**Say:** "This depends on your specific context..."
