# 💰 Personal Finance Tracker

_Comprehensive personal finance management_

## 🎯 Purpose

Track and manage personal finances:
- Income & expense tracking
- Budget management
- Financial goals
- Investment tracking
- Financial reports & analytics

## 💡 Features

### 1. **Expense Tracking**
- Auto-categorize expenses
- Manual entry support
- Receipt scanning (OCR)
- Recurring expenses

### 2. **Income Tracking**
- Salary & wages
- Freelance income
- Investment returns
- Passive income

### 3. **Budget Management**
- Monthly budgets
- Category budgets
- Budget alerts
- Overspending warnings

### 4. **Financial Goals**
- Savings goals
- Debt payoff goals
- Investment goals
- Progress tracking

### 5. **Reports & Analytics**
- Monthly summaries
- Category breakdowns
- Trend analysis
- Net worth tracking

## 💬 Usage

```
"Track expense: $50 for groceries"
"Show me my budget status"
"How much did I spend on dining this month?"
"Set savings goal: $10,000 for emergency fund"
"Show financial report for March"
```
